# Company set up
Before we begin setting up your company in Vithean, compile basic company information. It's best to have as many items from this checklist as possible, but you can always make changes later.

- **Country of your business**
- **Company name**
- **Legal structure of your business**
- **Your main principle business activity**
- **Email, Phone, Address**
- **Financial year, Accounting method**
- **Default language, Default currency**
- **Date format, Number format, Number format for input**

company set up preview, Check (Company Information, Company Type, Contact Information, Address, Accounts, Language/currency/Time Zone, Other Preference).
1. If any information is incorrect, click **Edit** to edit the information.

If you have a other chart of  accounts, click **Add**.

**Note** : Chart of accounts with lock cannot remove.

Chart of accounts preview, Check your chart of accounts.
